About

After years of experience in the recruitment industry, the founders of Wishes developed a solution for the significant labor shortages in the job market. The solution is an anonymous, digital job application document that allows employees to express their employment desires in more detail and with greater ease than ever before. The founders named this document the "Wishes profile".

The Wishes profile is designed to create a more transparent and beneficial relationship between employees and employers by providing insights into employees' employment preferences before the hiring process even begins. This transparency leads to more successful matches between employees and employers, reducing the likelihood of hiring mismatches that could lead to high turnover rates.

In addition, Wishes encourages employees to think more deeply about their employment desires and helps them better showcase their experience and skills to potential employers. By empowering employees and employers alike, the Wishes profile aims to improve the job market for everyone involved.

Upon sharing their job preferences, experience, and skills, users can receive job opportunities via their public wishes profile.
